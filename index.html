LORE

Why am I here?, What made me reach to this point?

It all started with being bored.. when I was in 7th grade, I really wanted to do something new, that I haven't done.. then randomly and youtube video showed up in my reccommended about Java, I was intrigued and I learnt java, I had made a simple calculator, after that I sometimes just made some random simple stuff in java and didn't think much of it till the last months of lockdown.. I was not having fun anymore, so I had the idea to make my own games that I can play with my friends to create my own fun, as I started making I realized the fun is not in the made but in the making, I found the positive feedback loop of coding to be very compulsive.. it got so bad that I code for fun, everyday whenever I get freetime I have atleast 2-3 active projects and I simply open Intellij or VS code and just make stuff, as well as playing games to not get burnt out.

After about 4 years now I think, I'm pretty well versed in Java, Python, HTML, CSS, Kinda JavaScript maybe..
I have worked on many projects, but as of right now I have 3 to show and the rest to be worked on.
